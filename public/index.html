Rent or Buy?

While I was deciding on whether to rent or buy, I was confused about all the variables that go into the decision. The common advice is to buy because

  • You're investing your money into something that always goes up
  • It's a safe investment
  • You're putting money into yourself and not someone else

I wasn't convinced as I knew my investments into index-based stocks and bonds also, in the long-run, always go up. I wanted some numbers to convince not just myself but others who kept nagging me about it.

Most of the calculators I've seen have been either

  • based on false assumptions, like staying at 1 rental the entire time, or
  • unclear on what the variables are for and how they're used (is this monthly? yearly? why a percentage and not a dollar amount?).

That's why I've created this calculator.

I've made this calculator primarily for myself. I'll try and keep it general but there's no guarantees. Fork it on GitHub if you want one for yourself!
